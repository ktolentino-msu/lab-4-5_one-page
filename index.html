About Kristen

Kristen is a graphic designer turned UX designer. She has a BFA in Graphic design from Auburn University, an M.A. in Mass communication, and is soon-to-be a graduate from the Michigan State University UX program. She currently lives in Charlotte, NC. In her spare time, she bakes and plans her move back to Michigan.
